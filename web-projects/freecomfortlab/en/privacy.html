Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Overview

FreeComfort Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our media conversion services.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT store your uploaded files
  • We do NOT keep conversion history
  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT track your usage patterns

How Our Service Works

All file conversions happen in real-time:

  • You upload a file
  • Our server converts it immediately
  • You download the converted file
  • The original and converted files are automatically deleted

Files are typically deleted within seconds after conversion.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for our core services. However, third-party advertising partners may use cookies as described above.

Security

While we take reasonable measures to protect data during transmission, please note that no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
